Willow Tree Events Privacy Policy

At Willow Tree Events, your privacy matters to us. This page explains our approach to privacy on www.willowtreeevents.co.uk and how it affects you.

Short Version

- We collect anonymous statistics about your visit, such as which pages you viewed. - Some third parties (e.g., Facebook, Instagram) may know you visited this site if you use their services. We don’t control this but don’t believe it poses any risk to you. - If you choose to share your information with us, we take great care to keep it secure and never share it with others without your explicit consent. - We only share your data with third parties when its necessary to provide our services.

Measuring Our Visitors

We use tools like Google Analytics to measure website visits. These tools collect information like the pages you visit and how you arrived at our site. All data collected is anonymous, meaning we don’t know who you are personally. This information helps us improve our website by understanding what visitors find most useful and how they navigate through our content.

Security

We take several steps to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, or unauthorized alteration. These include: - SSL encryption for secure data transmission. - Secured servers located in data centers with firewall protection. - Access to information is restricted via passwords and secure keys. - Regular monitoring to ensure the safety of stored data. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee the security of data sent over the internet.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our site. We use cookies for the following purposes: - Identifying returning users and subscribers. - Simplifying the login process for returning visitors. - Tracking user behavior to improve our site and services. - Building an anonymous profile based on your browsing patterns, which helps us provide more relevant content. You can delete cookies from your device anytime, but doing so may affect certain features, like saved login details or site preferences. You can still use our website if you disable cookies, though some content or functionality may be limited. By continuing to use our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described here.

Contacting Us

When you contact us, we may collect personal information such as your name and email address. This information is stored securely and used only for responding to your inquiries or improving our services.

Emails

From time to time, we may send you emails, such as newsletters or updates. You have the option to opt out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email. We will never share your email address or personal information with third parties unless necessary to deliver our services.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time, especially as new technologies emerge. You can always check this page for the most recent version.
